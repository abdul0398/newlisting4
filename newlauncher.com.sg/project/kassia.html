Kassssia

Situated in the serene enclave of Flora Drive, Kassia is a prestigious freehold new launch condo developed by Tripartite Developers Pte Ltd, a consortium of Hong Leong Holdings, City Developments, and TID. This luxurious development features 276 exquisitely designed units, complemented by top-notch facilities including a grand clubhouse and a 50m lap pool. Kassia enjoys excellent connectivity with easy access to Changi Airport, Pasir Ris, and Tampines via the Pan Island Expressway (PIE) and Tampines Expressway (TPE). The nearby Tampines East MRT station, just a short bus ride away, provides direct access to the city center through the Downtown Line (DTL). Families will benefit from the proximity to reputable schools such as White Sands Primary and East Spring Primary. With shopping destinations like White Sands and Jewel Changi Airport close by, Kassia offers an ideal blend of tranquil living and modern convenience. Read More

Kassia Price List

Priced between $985,000 for a 1 Bedroom unit and $2.7M for a 4 Bedroom unit, the price per square foot (PSF) for Kassia ranges from $1,830 to $2,169. Refer to the Balance Units section below for the latest pricing of the available units in Kassia.

Kassia Showflat

Kassia showflat is located along Eunos Avenue 4 and is open for viewing by appointment only. Keen to know more about this new launch? Book your viewing with our sales team to visit the exclusive Kassia showflat today.

Kassia is developed by Tripartite Developers, comprising Hong Leong and CDL, who are noted for their track record of successful residential projects in the Changi North area.

Kassia is conveniently located close to a variety of educational institutions, including Temasek Junior College, Dunman Secondary School, Ngee Ann Secondary School, East Spring Primary/Secondary School, and White Sands Primary School, making it a practical choice for families seeking accessible education options.

Kassia's location is advantageous for professionals, being close to several major employment centers such as Changi Business Park, Changi Aviation Park, Tampines Regional Centre, and the upcoming Changi Airport Terminal 5, offering substantial career opportunities nearby.

Developers Profile

Hong Leong Holdings Limited (HLHL) was established in 1968 as the privately-held property development and investment arm of the Hong Leong Group. As one of the pioneers of the real estate scene in Singapore, HLHL has since emerged as a major player in the property market. As a forward-looking developer that continues to pursue improvement and expansion while maintaining its high design, customer service and sustainability standards, HLHL takes pride in being responsible for some of the most distinctive and iconic residential developments in Singapore.

City Developments Limited is inspiring the future of real estate globally. As a leading global real estate company based in Singapore, City Developments Limited has a network spanning 112 locations in 29 countries and regions. Listed on the Singapore Exchange, City Developments Limited has a proven track record and boasts over 55 years in real estate development, investment and management. City Developments Limited has developed over 47,000 homes, with recent projects including Irwell Hill Residences, Sengkang Grand Residences and Amber Park.

Established in 1972, TID Pte. Ltd. is a joint venture between property developers, Hong Leong Group and Mitsui Fudosan, weaving a unique combination of Singapore-Japanese talents and management styles to drive excellence in real estate developments. In collaboration with partners, TID Pte. Ltd. has successfully executed a total of 36 residential projects, including a diverse portfolio of commercial, industrial and hotel properties.
