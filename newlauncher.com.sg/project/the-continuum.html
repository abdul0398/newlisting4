The Continuum

Nestled within the vibrant heritage district in Katong, The Continuum is a freehold new launch condo development comprising 816 units across six apartment blocks of 17-18 storeys. Sitting on a site spanning over 250,000 square feet, it is one of the largest new residential projects in the area, boosting a wide range of facilities spread across the North and South buildings. This includes a multitude of social spaces from sky gardens to pavilions, lap pools and family pools, as well as a conserved Thiam Siew Clubhouse right in the middle of the compound. Here, residents can experience the best of both worlds from the cultural-rich neighbourhood and the modern luxury within. Located along Haig Road, it is within 1km from both Paya Lebar MRT interchange and Dakota MRT station, providing easy access to City Hall and downtown Singapore. Also within 1km are prominent schools such as Tanjong Katong Primary/Secondary School, Tanjong Katong Girls’ School and Chung Cheng High School, making this development a conducive choice for families with school-going children. The Continuum Showflat

The Continuum showflat is located beside Dunman Regency along Dunman Road, but is open for viewing by appointment only. Keen to learn more about this property? Book an appointment with the sales team to visit The Continuum showflat today.

Situated on an expansive site, offering a wide array of facilities from ground to top level, such as lap pools, jacuzzi pools, hot tub, sky gardens and sky gyms.

Stunning views of the East Coast beachfront from the well-equipped roof terrace, consisting of a grill deck, community garden and an outdoor lounge.

Islandwide connectivity with Dakota MRT station (Circle Line) and Paya Lebar MRT interchange (East-West and Circle Lines) just a 10-minute walk away.

Developers Profile

Founded in Singapore in 1983, Hoi Hup Realty has developed a strong reputation as a real estate developer with a well-diversified portfolio of developments. Leading the way with projects consisting of executive & private condominiums, landed housings and mixed-use commercial buildings, Hoi Hup has delivered over 7,300 high-quality homes and the landmark 33-storey Royal Square.

Since being founded in 1974 in Malaysia, Sunway Group has become a top real estate developer and one of the largest conglomerates worldwide. Sunway Group’s unique Build-Own-Operate model enables it to be an integral collaborator with communities where they operate. Landmark projects include Sunway City Kuala Lumpur and Sunway City Ipoh.
