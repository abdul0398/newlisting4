Lentor Hills Residences

Lentor Hills Residences is a 99-year leasehold new launch condominium that consists of 598 residential units housed within 5 residential blocks, with one prominently located at 31 Lentor Hills Road, Singapore 788881. Spanning a sprawling site area of 184,460 square feet, the development offers a spacious vicinity where residents can enjoy a wide range of resort-style facilities. These include an infinity swimming pool, jacuzzi, onsen alcove, well-furnished play zone and work zone clubhouses, a sky spa cove with jacuzzi, and various dining pavilions. Nestled in a serene neighbourhood and surrounded by parks and nature reserves, residents will appreciate the peace and quiet of their private enclave. Conveniently situated just a five-minute walk from Lentor MRT station, Lentor Hills Residences provides easy access to the city via the Thomson-East Coast Line (TEL). Moreover, the proximity to primary to tertiary educational institutions, such as Anderson Primary School, Presbyterian High School, CHIJ St. Nicholas Girls’ School, Anderson Serangoon Junior College, and Nanyang Polytechnic, within 1-2km away, makes it an ideal location for families with school-going children. The URA Master Plan and Lentor Hills Residences

This strategic vision by the URA aims to rejuvenate and enhance well-established neighbourhoods like Ang Mo Kio, bringing new housing projects, amenities, and community spaces to create more livable and inclusive communities.

The URA Draft Master Plan 2019 particularly highlights the development of the Lentor Hills neighbourhood, where Lentor Hills Residences is located. The Lentor MRT station on the TEL, situated conveniently close to Lentor Hills Residences, promises easy access to various parts of Singapore, reducing commute times and making travel more efficient.
