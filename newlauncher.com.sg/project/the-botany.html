The Botany

Immerse in the quiet sanctuary of The Botany, a 99-year leasehold new condo development consisting of 386 units, spread across 5 blocks of 9 to 15 storey buildings. Across Dairy Farm Road, nature-loving residents can take a scenic forest walk to Dairy Farm Nature Park, which connects to Bukit Timah Nature Reserve. Despite being located at the city fringe, off Upper Bukit Timah Road, residents can reach Hillview MRT station by foot in about 10 minutes, effectively linking them to downtown areas such as Bugis and Marina Bay via the Downtown Line (DTL). For more dining options, residents can also head to the nearby HillV2 or The Rail Mall, both within a 15-minute walk away. Schools like GESS International School, CHIJ Our Lady Queen Of Peace and Assumption English School are also within walking distance. Read More

The Botany Price List

Priced between $2.048M for a 3 Bedroom unit and $2.566M for a 3 Bedroom + Study unit, the price per square foot (PSF) for The Botany ranges from $1,956 to $2,350. Refer to the Balance Units section below for the latest pricing of the available units in The Botany.

The Botany Showflat

The Botany showflat is located at 50A Dairy Farm Walk, but is open for viewing by appointment only. Keen to learn more about this property? Book an appointment with the sales team to visit The Botany showflat today.

Enjoy fresher, cooler air, surrounded by lush greenery from the nearby parks and reserves, such as Dairy Farm Nature Park and Bukit Timah Nature Reserve.

Within a 1km walk from Hillview MRT station, connecting residents to downtown locations like Bugis and the Central Business District via Downtown Line (DTL).

Close proximity to schools like GESS International School, CHIJ Our Lady Queen Of Peace and Assumption English School, which is convenient for families with school-going children.
