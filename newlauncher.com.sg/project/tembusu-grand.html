Tembusu Grand

Immerse in the laidback charm of the Katong heritage district at Tembusu Grand - a 99-year leasehold new launch condominium housing a total of 638 apartments across four blocks of 20-21 storeys . Other than having a breathtaking view of the sea and a short drive to the beach at East Coast Park, residents also have access to a wide range of amenities both within the development and from nearby shopping malls like Parkway Parade and Katong Shopping Centre. Designed to cater to different lifestyles, Tembusu Grand is equipped with shared social spaces like the clubhouse, outdoor pavilions and BBQ pits for gatherings, state-of-the-art facilities such as gymnasium, tennis courts and swimming pools to support healthy living, and also dedicated children playground and play pool for families. With the upcoming Tanjong Katong MRT station just a short stroll away, residents can easily connect to Orchard Road and downtown Singapore. Families with school-going children can also enjoy the convenience of having popular schools like Tanjong Katong Primary/Secondary School, Tanjong Katong Girls’ School, Canadian International School and Chung Cheng High School in close proximity. Tembusu Grand Price List

Priced between $1.384M for a 1 Bedroom + Study unit and $7.888M for a 5 Bedroom Penthouse unit, the price per square foot (PSF) for Tembusu Grand ranges from $2,174 to $2,931. Refer to the Balance Units section below for the latest pricing of the available units in Tembusu Grand.

Tembusu Grand Showflat

The Tembusu Grand showflat is located near the project site at 11 Jalan Gajus, but is open for viewing by appointment only. Keen to learn more about this new launch property? Book an appointment with the sales team to visit the exclusive Tembusu Grand showflat today.

Located in an idyllic, heritage district of Katong, near shopping malls like Parkway Parade and Katong Shopping Centre, as well as the beach at East Coast Park.

Enjoy breezy seaside living with a panoramic view of the sea from the balcony.

Walking distance from Tanjong Pagar MRT station, allowing residents to connect to Orchard Road and downtown Singapore easily via the Thomson-East Coast Line (TEL).
